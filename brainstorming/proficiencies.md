# Proficiencies

Proficiencies indicate how well an entity will use a particular weapon. This means the damage output, and in the future attack chances. They also effect different enemies differently based on the PRPSS system (Proficiency Rock Paper Scissors System)

## Competency
- 0: Incompetent, heavy weapon penalty
- 1: In-experienced, moderate weapon penalty
- 2: Average, no weapon penalty or bonus
- 3: Trained, slight weapon bonus
- 4: Experienced, moderate weapon bonus
- 5: Mastery, heavy weapon bonus
